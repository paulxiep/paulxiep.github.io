## Pixie

Visit Pixie's home page at [pixiepavel.com](https://pixiepavel.com)

Pixie: Magical, whimsical, and mischievous fairy in British folklore. 

It also encodes a name that holds personal meaning to me.

### Effortless Elegance

As a brand, it evokes the image of magic and effortlessness. Secondarily, of beauty and playfulness. Therefore, *Effortless Elegance* is the first tagline I came up with to communicates what Pixie is.

### Pixie Space

[Pixie Space](https://space.pixiepavel.com) is [Pixie](https://pixiepavel.com)'s first product that aims to simplify the workflow of Technical Designing in fashion industry.
